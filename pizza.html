Pizza Recipe

This is a recipe for pizza.

Make a tasty pizza with this simple recipe.

Gather ingredients and then get cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mozarella cheese
  • 1 cup pepperoni
  • 1 16" pre-made crust
  • 1 cup black olives
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Unwrap and place pre-made crust on baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce onto crust, outward from center in a circular motion.
  4. Add shredded mozarella, pepperoni, olives.
  5. Place in oven and bake for 10-12 minutes at 450 F.
  6. Remove from oven, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with Parmesan.
  7. Let cool for 5-10 minutes and Enjoy.
